**Steam TCP/IPC/URI Fuzzing Tools**

These scripts, dictionary, and harness were created to map and attack the surface of Valve Steam tools for the purpose of bug bounty. They can serve as a stub for further research and expansion, or as a historical resource indicating the thorough testing of the Steam attack surface!
